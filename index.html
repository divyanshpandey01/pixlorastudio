Simple Workflow

Six steps. Zero friction.

From your camera roll to your client's phone in under a minute. Here's the complete journey.

1

Photographer Uploads

Drag and drop your edited event photos into the Pixlora dashboard. Bulk upload thousands in minutes.

2

QR Code Generated

A branded QR code and shareable link are automatically created for your event gallery.

3

Guest Scans QR

Guests at the event simply scan the QR code with their phone camera — no app download needed.

4

Upload a Selfie

The guest takes or uploads a quick selfie. This is the magic key to finding their photos.

5

AI Finds Matches

Our AI scans thousands of photos and instantly finds every image featuring that guest's face.

6

Download Instantly

All matched photos are presented in a personal gallery. Download in full resolution with one tap.
